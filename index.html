About Us

Wissenaire stands out as the premier Annual Techno-Management Fest at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar, offering a diverse range of events in the realms of Science, Technology, and Management. Featuring a dynamic lineup of Competitions, Workshops, Exhibitions, Star nights, Quizzes, Colloquia, and Lectures, Wissenaire serves as a platform to ignite the spirit of technical excellence, creativity, and innovation among young minds. It is revered as one of the most anticipated Fests in Eastern India, captivating participants and spectators alike with its vibrant and enriching programs.
